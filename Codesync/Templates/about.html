Revolutionizing Code Collaboration

We're building the future where AI and human creativity combine to create extraordinary software, making coding more collaborative, intelligent, and accessible for developers worldwide.

Our Mission

To democratize software development by combining the power of artificial intelligence with real-time collaboration, enabling developers of all skill levels to build better software faster, together. We believe that the best code is written when human creativity meets AI intelligence.

Our Story

Born from Developer Frustration

CodeSync AI was born in 2023 when our founders, experienced software engineers from leading tech companies, faced the same frustrations developers encounter daily: fragmented collaboration tools, repetitive debugging, and the constant context switching between different platforms.

We envisioned a world where coding would be as collaborative as working on a Google Doc, as intelligent as having a senior developer by your side, and as seamless as having all your tools in one place.

Today, CodeSync AI serves over 50,000 developers across 120+ countries, helping teams ship code 3x faster while reducing bugs by 90%. We're just getting started.
